Best Danish Theory Test App 2025: Compare Your Options

There are several apps and platforms for preparing for the Danish theory test. Here's an honest comparison to help you choose the right one.

What to Look For

When choosing a theory test preparation app, the key factors are:

  • English availability — can you study in English?
  • Pricing model — one-time or subscription?
  • Offline access — can you practice without internet?
  • Test format — does it match the real exam?
  • Progress tracking — can you see your weak areas?
  • Platform — iOS, Android, web?

The Options

KørApp

Price: 99 DKK (one-time purchase)

Platform: iOS

English: Yes — full English support, switchable to Danish

Offline: Yes — all content downloaded to phone

Test format: Matches real exam (25 scenarios, yes/no, 25-minute timer)

Progress tracking: Yes — tracks performance by topic

Strengths: Most affordable option. Clean, modern app. Full English support. No subscription — pay once, use forever.

Limitations: iOS only (no Android or web version currently).

Teoriklar.dk

Price: Varies (subscription-based)

Platform: Web (desktop, tablet, mobile browser)

English: Yes

Offline: No — web-based, requires internet

Test format: Similar to real exam

Strengths: Well-established platform. Good question bank. Audio read-aloud feature.

Limitations: Web-only (no native app). Subscription model means ongoing cost.

Prove.dk

Price: Varies (packages from ~199 DKK)

Platform: Web

English: Yes — supports 25 languages

Offline: No

Test format: 40+ practice tests

Strengths: Huge question bank. Video lessons included. Supports many languages beyond English and Danish. 100,000+ users.

Limitations: Web-only. More expensive. The large feature set can be overwhelming if you just want practice tests.

Teoriundervisning.dk

Price: From 79 DKK

Platform: Web + app

English: English theory book available

Offline: Partially (app features)

Strengths: Includes a theory book, not just tests. Good for people who want structured learning.

Limitations: Less focused on test practice. More of a learning platform than a test simulator.

Drive4you.dk

Price: Varies

Platform: Web (works on all devices)

English: Yes

Offline: No

Strengths: Uses the same digital test design as Færdselsstyrelsen. Cross-device progress (start on phone, continue on computer).

Limitations: Web-only. Requires internet connection.

Our Recommendation

We're obviously biased (this site is powered by KørApp), so here's our honest take:

  • Best value for money: KørApp at 99 DKK one-time. No subscription, full English, offline access, and a 4.8 App Store rating
  • Most comprehensive: Prove.dk if you want video lessons and support for many languages beyond English
  • Best for structured learning: Teoriundervisning.dk if you want a theory book alongside practice tests

If you have an iPhone and want the most affordable way to practice for the theory test in English, KørApp is the clear choice. If you're on Android, you'll need to use one of the web-based platforms.

The real cost comparison: At 99 DKK, KørApp costs less than a single extra driving lesson (~400–600 DKK) and much less than a theory test retake (600 DKK). It's the highest-ROI investment in the entire driving licence process.
